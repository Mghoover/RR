How to use Ruff Rub

  1. Clean and dry paws.
  2. Warm a small amount between fingers.
  3. Massage into paw pads (and between toes if needed).
  4. Allow a minute to sink in before activity or bedtime.
Tips:
  • Apply before walks on hot pavement, snow, or rough trails.
  • Use daily for very dry/cracked pads, then 2–3× weekly for maintenance.
  • Great for noses and elbow calluses, too.

FAQ

Is Ruff Rub safe if my dog licks their paws?

Yes. Ruff Rub uses food‑grade oils and waxes with a light 1% frankincense essential oil. Always supervise your pet and discontinue use if irritation occurs.
